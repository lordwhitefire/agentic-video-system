# Law 10: No Watermarked Images

After generating ANY graphic or video that contains an image, extract a MIDDLE frame and VLM-check for watermarks on the image element BEFORE committing.

**Applies to:** Editor agent, Researcher agent.
**Forbidden sources:** Alamy, Getty Images, Shutterstock, SSRS, any image with photographer credits or URL overlays.
**Required:** VLM check on the RENDERED OUTPUT, not just the source image.
